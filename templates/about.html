Our Mission

Pizza Store is a web application developed for the CS50 final project. It allows users to browse in a catalog of different pizzas, add to a cart, in the cart shows the total value of the order and the preparation time. The system is built using Flask, Python, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and SQLite.

Our goal is to provide a user-friendly and responsive interface, making the pizza ordering experience simple and enjoyable.
